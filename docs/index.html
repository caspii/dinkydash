Why families love DinkyDash

No more answering the same questions. Your family just looks at the screen.

📅

Calendar at a glance

Syncs with Google Calendar so everyone knows what's happening today, without asking.

🔄

Fair chore rotation

Automatically rotates daily chores between family members. No arguments, no forgetting.

⏱

Countdowns to the fun stuff

Birthdays, holidays, vacations. Kids always know how many days until the next big thing.

🤖

AI-written surprises

Fun facts, daily challenges, and personalized messages — all different, every single day.

📸

Tiny screen, big smiles

Designed for a small display on a Raspberry Pi. Mount it in the kitchen and everyone sees it.

⚙️

Easy to set up

One YAML file to configure your family, pets, chores, and dates. No accounts, no cloud, no fuss.

How it works

1

Describe your family

Add names, birthdays, pets, chores, and your Google Calendar link to a simple config file.

2

AI creates your dashboard each morning

Every day at 6am, DinkyDash sends your family's info to Claude, which writes a complete, personalized dashboard.

3

Display it on a small screen

A Raspberry Pi serves the dashboard in the kitchen, hallway, or wherever your family gathers. It refreshes automatically.

Free and open source

DinkyDash is MIT-licensed. Set it up in an afternoon and have a new dashboard waiting for your family every morning.
