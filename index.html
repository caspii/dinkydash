DinkyDash

Create simple emoji-based dashboards. Display then on any internet-capable device.

Perfect for families with kids

DinkyDash is great for quickly answering those questions that kids like to ask again and again and again and again.

  • "How many days till Christmas?"
  • "Who's turn is it to take the trash out?"
  • "When is my birthday party?"
See an example

Great for a Raspberry Pi

This is an ideal project for the famous mini computer. Create your own custom dashboard and mount it in your hall or kitchen.

Very easy to setup

Creating your own dashboard takes less than 5 minuted. If you don't believe us, give it a try.
